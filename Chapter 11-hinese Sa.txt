The National Highway Traffic Safety Administration is investigating a vehicle that crashed into a police barricade at San Francisco's Embarcadero Friday.

The crash happened shortly after 4 p.m. near the Embarcadero and was captured on video by the SFPD.

The vehicle, which was a black Chevrolet Malibu, was traveling eastbound on Yonge Street around 4:30 p.m. when it "took off from the sidewalk and crashed into the barricade," the SFPD said in a news release.

The driver was taken to a local hospital for treatment, the announcement said.

The victim, whose name has not been released, was taken by ambulance to a local hospital.

The crash caused $6,300 in damage, and the vehicle was towed back to the scene of the crash, the release said.
