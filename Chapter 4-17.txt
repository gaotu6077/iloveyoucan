Paleontologists uncover a nearly complete skull of a 30 million-year-old apex carnivore in Egypt - CNN reports.

An ancient skull found in an ancient Egyptian tomb that's the first to be discovered in the world shows a dinosaur with a skull with teeth covered in a skull cap.

The fossilised skull of the giant carnivore, known as the "Crocodile Goliath" - which is extinct - is found in the ancient Pyramids of Giza.
